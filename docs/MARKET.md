# Market Analysis

Method + assumptions (market-research skill). **All sizing is proxy Fermi estimation**
pending sourced validation — every number travels with its method.

## Sizing (TAM/SAM/SOM)

| Metric | Top-down | Bottoms-up | Read |
|--------|----------|------------|------|
| TAM | $8M | $12M | small market |
| SAM | $2.8M | $3.0M | converge |
| SOM (3yr) | $112k | $120k | **tight: ~$116k/yr** |

- Top-down TAM $8M = Nepali slice of South Asian caption-SaaS market (proxy).
- Bottoms-up = 400k candidate creators × $30/yr (both **unsourced**; see below).
- **Triangulation FAILED (50% TAM gap)** — driven by uncertainty in creator count + WTP.
  SOM converges tightly, so the go/no-go conclusion is robust to the gap.

## Verdict

**Micro-SaaS / lifestyle-business scale, NOT VC-scale.** SOM ceiling ~$100–150k/yr ARR
near-term. Fine for solo bootstrap; do not raise money against it. To grow TAM beyond
~$12M → must expand past Nepali-only (multi-language). "Nepali-first" is a *wedge*.
